what is BMI (BODY MASS INDEX) ?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple and reliable tool used to estimate whether your body weight is healthy for your height. It calculates a number using your weight and height to categorize you into ranges such as underweight, normal, overweight, or obese.

BMI is widely used by healthcare professionals to assess your risk for conditions like heart disease, diabetes, and more. While it does not directly measure body fat, it gives a quick insight into whether you are within a healthy range or may need to make lifestyle adjustments.
